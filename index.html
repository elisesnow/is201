About Elise

Elise Snow

Elise has been singing lead vocals in local cover bands for the last 8 years. She was born, raise and currently lives in the south Salt Lake Valley. She comes from a strong musical background in musical theatre and has been trained in mulitple vocal styles. Elise has the voice of an angel, so say her family and friends.
